General

The Tarot is a deck of 78 cards: 22 major arcana cards and 56 minor arcana cards with 4 suits which are cups, wands, swords, and pentacles. The major arcana represent lifeâ€™s karmic and spiritual lessons while the minor arcana reflect the trials and tribulations that we experience daily.
Each of these cards are symbolism of imagery, symbolism and story of our own experiences. Consider it as a personal storybook and mirror to our soul.

Cups

The suit of Cups is associated with the element of water. It deals with our emotions, relationships, inner life, and spirituality. It also indicates that you are thinking with your heart and not your head. Finally, it links with creativity, romaticism, fantasy, and imagination.
The negative sides of the card are over-emotional or completely disengaged and dispassionate, having unrealisitc expectations and fantasies, repressed emotions, an inability to express oneself, and lack of creativity.

Pentacles

The suit of Pentacles is associated with the element of earth. It deals with physical and external level of consciousness. Therefore mirrors your health, finances, work, and creativity. It also associated with ego, self-esteem, and self-image. The material world is linked with this suit. The positive aspect of this card are manifestation, realization, and proof and prosperity.
The negative sides of the card are being possesive, greedy, overly materialistic, over-indulging, not exercising, not effectively managing finances, and being overly focused on career that is hurting other aspects of the person's life.

Swords

The suit of Swords is associated with the element of air. It deals with mental level of consciousness and surrounded by mind and intellect. It mirrors your thoughts, attitudes, and beliefs. The symbolism is a fine balance between spirit (wands) and feeling (cups) as its positive effects, especially power and intellect.
The negative effects of the card are anger, guilt, harsh judgment, a lack of compassion, and verbal and mental abuse.

Wands

The suit of Wands is associated with the element of fire. It deals with primal energy, spirituality, inspiration, determination, strength, intuition, creativity, ambition and expansion, original thought and the seeds through which life brings forth. It mirrors what is important to you at the core of your being. It's your personality, ego, enthusiasm, self-concept, and personal energy both internally and externally.
The negative effects of the card are illusion, egotistical behavior, impulsiveness, a lack of direction or purpose, or feeling meaningless.
